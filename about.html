Our Mission

The Future of
Hair is Fluid.

We started Canvas because we were tired of the binary. We believe that a haircut shouldn't be defined by who sits in the chair, but by the hair on their head.

Hourly Pricing

Historically, women have paid more for "short cuts" than men. We removed gender from the equation. You pay for the time you book. 45 minutes costs the same for everyone.

Gratuity Free

Tipping culture is confusing and rooted in inequality. We pay our staff a professional, livable wage so they don't rely on tips. The price on the menu is the final price.

Sensory Friendly

Salons can be loud and overwhelming. We offer "Silent Appointments" and use fragrance-free products upon request to ensure everyone feels comfortable.

Eco-Conscious

We are a Green Circle certified salon. We recycle 95% of our waste, including hair clippings, foils, and color tubes.

"Come As You Are."

Canvas is more than a salon. It is a community space. Whether you are transitioning, maintaining, or transforming, you are safe here.
